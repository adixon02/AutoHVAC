Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about AutoHVAC. Can't find the answer you're looking for? Contact our support team.

How accurate are the Manual J calculations? +
Our calculations are ACCA Manual J compliant and have been validated against thousands of existing designs. We achieve 95%+ accuracy compared to manual calculations while eliminating human error.
What file formats can I upload for blueprints? +
We support PDF, PNG, JPG, DWG, and DXF files. Our AI can extract room dimensions and layout details from architectural drawings, floor plans, and even hand-drawn sketches.
Do you support commercial projects? +
Currently we focus on residential and light commercial projects up to 10,000 sq ft. Commercial-grade features including Manual N and advanced zoning are coming in Q2 2024.
How does local code compliance work? +
We automatically detect climate zones by ZIP code and apply local building codes and energy requirements. Our database covers 95% of US jurisdictions with regular updates for code changes.
Can I customize the reports with my company branding? +
Yes! Professional and Enterprise plans include custom branding options. Add your logo, company colors, and contact information to all generated reports.
